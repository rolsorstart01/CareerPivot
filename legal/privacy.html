Privacy Policy

At CareerPivot, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data:

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide (name, email, career details) and technical data (cookies, IP address) via Firebase and Google Authentication.

2. Use of Information

Your career data is used solely to generate personalized analyses. Your email is used for account management and important service updates.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Razorpay for payment processing and do not store your credit card or banking details on our servers.

5. Your Rights

You may request a copy of your data or request account deletion by contacting our support team.
